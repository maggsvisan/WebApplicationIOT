Mission

The objective of this project is to develop a web application along with a controller that will make more efficient the energy consumption of the classrooms at ITESM Campus Monterrey. This application will provide the users an easy way to control the classroom lights and temperature remotely.


Vision

To provide information and control to the users about a classroom lights and temperature at anytime, just by having Internet connection.
